About Me

I am Saeid Taleghanidoozdoozan (Taleghani), a Postdoctoral Researcher at the AI + Remote Sensing Lab, working across the University of Waterloo and the University of Calgary, Canada.

My research focuses on the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Earth Observation. I design and implement deep learning architectures — particularly CNNs and Transformers — to solve complex geospatial problems including land cover classification, change detection, sea ice monitoring, and urban analysis.

I have extensive experience with both optical imagery (Landsat, Sentinel-2, MODIS) and SAR data (Sentinel-1, TerraSAR-X), and I leverage platforms like Google Earth Engine to process large-scale satellite datasets efficiently.
